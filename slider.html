Ghost Buddy Roadtrip Contest

Win a Ghost Buddy NFT! Up to 3 winners!

The Ghost Buddy Roadtrip Contest is a community driven contest where the community decides the winner. The contest is split into two leagues, the Community League and the Ghost League. The Community League is open to all members of the Ghost Buddy community who hold one or more Buddies. The Ghost League is open to everybody who do not has a Ghost Buddy NFT yet. The winner of each league will receive a Ghost Buddy NFT. The 3th Ghost Buddy NFT will be awarded to community's free pick (All submissions will be elidgable for this prize)
